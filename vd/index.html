DANIELLE CASADO, CD WORKSHOP, 1/31/2025
     A PERSONAL INVENTORY VISUALIZED

This Data Map plots objects of emotional significance. Each object has varying levels of attachment, usage, and emotional output, as decided by the designer. The map presents a top down view of the designer's room, where they keep these objects, and plots where each object would reside, as long as how often they are interacted with, how attached the designer is to them, and what emotion they envoke in the designer.
